About

The story

Eoin Gibbons is a PGA professional based at Kinsealy Grange Golf Academy in North Dublin. Eoin started his PGA Training @Kilkenny golf club under PGA Professional Jimmy Bolger, From there Eoin started coaching at two locations Tinhalla in Carrick on Suir and Naas Driving Range in Kildare and grew a reputation for lowering scores and handicaps. After a number of years Eoin moved to his current base Kinsealy Grange and is currently coach to a number of Elite players from Fellow Professionals to Irish Internationals and interpros. Eoin coaches all levels of golfers and uses some of the best technology available such as Trackman, Live View golf and video.
